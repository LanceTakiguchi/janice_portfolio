Typeface Poster

A typographic poster based on the serif typeface called "Baskerville," designed by John Baskerville in 1757. Color theme includes dark purple and blues for an added richness effect. This poster showcases the entire lowercase and uppercase alphabet, as well all the numbers of Baskerville. Special feautured letters include uppercase Q, H, and an ampersand.

Legacy Poster

This poster features the legacy left behind by Paul Rand, a graphic designer who lived from 1914-1996. Paul Rand's corportate logo designs are highlighted in this poster as he is best known them. They include: IBM, UPS, Enron, Morningstar, Inc., Westinghouse, ABC, NeXT, and more.

Type Hierarchy Poster 1

A typographic poster that highlights the sans-serif font "Univers," designed by Adrian Frutiger in 1954. This poster's informational content is entirely fictional. It showcases a lecture series that might have been held at the University of California Davis campus by famous designers.

Book Cover Design

This cover design is a showcase into what the 33-pages of this book look like. This book's design uses the font-family of "Frutiger." The informational content inside is based upon excerpts from Paul Rand's article titled: "Design and the Play Instinct."
